About us

Founded in 2022 in Sibiu, Romania, AirULBS website's goal is to grow from a small application to one of the world's leading digital travel companies. The mission for this application is to make it easier for everyone to experience the world focusing on the main events of the year. By investing in the technology that helps take the friction out of travel, AirULBS seamlessly connects millions of travellers with memorable experiences, a range of transport options and incredible places to stay in the best hotels of the world.
